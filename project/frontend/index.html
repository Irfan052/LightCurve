Detailed Science Processing Sequence

Below are the intermediate stages generated by the scientific processing pipeline. You can review the raw values, detrending filter fitting, and the Box Least Squares (BLS) periodogram power spectrum.

1. Raw Observational Light Curve

Initial time-series downloaded directly from MAST. May contain gaps, long-term trends, cosmic ray spikes, and instrumental systematics.

2. Box Least Squares (BLS) Periodogram

Power spectrum search across candidate trial periods. The vertical dashed line shows the peak frequency matching the orbital period candidate.
